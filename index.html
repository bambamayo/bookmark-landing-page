Features

Our aim is to make it quick amd easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks anyhow you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that can send at the click of a button
